Edel Et Omno

Edel Et Omno is a personal worldbuilding project, which I've been working on for many years on and off. Inspired by the greats like Game Of Thrones, Dune & Lord Of The Rings, it spans a vast magical world, which features a complex, rich network of different societies, cultures and characters.

Order of the Mountains

The Order of the Mountain is located in the South-East and is most well know for it's iconic mountain skyline, featuring the three peaks of the most important and sacred mountains. Most well know for their cold and harsh climate, the people of the mountains tend to be defined by tradition, rational, and a determined spirit to previal amidst any conflicts. Their territory borders the vast desert in the center of this continent which creates a stark contrast in their landscapes. At their most western point, they oversea any trade and travel coming from the south and play a vital role when it comes to crossing the desert from east to west,
